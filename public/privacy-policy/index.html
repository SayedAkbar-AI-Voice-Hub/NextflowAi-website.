Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

AI Voice Hub Inc (“we,” “us,” “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, services, and technology solutions, including SMS marketing, appointment reminders, voice AI agents, lead generation websites, and automated business communication systems.

By using our services or opting into our communications, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms and Conditions.

2. Information We Collect

Contact Information

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you submit forms, book appointments, request information, interact with our websites or landing pages, or otherwise provide it to us.

Communications Data

If you opt in, we collect your messaging preferences and maintain records of consent and opt-out activity related to SMS, email, and voice AI communications.

Usage and Device Information

We may use cookies, pixels, and analytics tools to collect information about how you use our websites and client websites we build and manage. This may include your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction data.

Lead and Inquiry Data

When you interact with websites, forms, chat widgets, or voice AI agents built or operated by us, we may collect the information you provide during those interactions, including inquiries, service requests, and appointment details.

Voice AI Interaction Data

If you interact with our AI-powered voice agents, we may collect call recordings, transcriptions, and interaction data to provide and improve our services. You will be notified when interacting with an AI agent.

Appointment and Service Data

If you schedule appointments or use our services or our clients’ services, we may collect details associated with those interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide requested services, customer support, and lead management
  • Send appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Send SMS and email communications that you have explicitly consented to receive
  • Facilitate AI-powered voice agent interactions for scheduling, qualification, and customer service
  • Capture and route leads through websites, forms, and automated systems we build and manage
  • Improve our websites, services, and marketing performance using analytics
  • Maintain compliance records related to consent and opt-outs
  • Protect our business and users, including fraud and security monitoring

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to personalize your experience and understand how our websites are used. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, or consent information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted vendors who help us deliver services, such as SMS and email delivery platforms, voice AI providers, CRM systems, scheduling tools, website hosting, and analytics providers. These providers may only use information to perform services on our behalf.

Client Businesses

When we operate lead generation websites, voice AI agents, or automated systems on behalf of our business clients, the information you provide may be shared with the relevant client business for the purpose of fulfilling your inquiry or service request.

Legal and Safety

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of AI Voice Hub Inc, our users, or others.

Business Transfers

If AI Voice Hub Inc is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred as part of that transaction. Any successor will be required to honor this Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Sharing Restrictions

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

6. Your Choices

SMS Opt-Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send STOP, we will send a confirmation message and you will no longer receive SMS messages unless you opt back in.

Email Opt-Out

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email.

Voice AI Interactions

You may request to speak with a human representative at any time during a voice AI interaction. You may also request that we delete recordings or transcriptions of your interactions.

Access and Correction

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable law.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
